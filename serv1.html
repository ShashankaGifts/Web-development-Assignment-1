Event booking software that lets you manage your venue spaces with ease

Whatever types of events your venue books - from meetings and conferences to social events like weddings and parties - Planning Pod gives you the tools to track all the details related to reserving your rentable spaces. It's an event booking system and much more, giving you both space scheduling tools as well as other tools for room layout / event floorplan designs as well as productivity - 26+ tools in all to automate many tasks for your venue.

Scheduling and calendaring tools so you never double book

It's vital that you know exactly which event room or space you have booked, when you have booked it and to whom. Our online booking software provides extensive event calendaring and scheduling tools so you can account for all your event spaces and schedule bookings with fields for date/time, client, pricing, setup details and much more. As venue booking software goes, it covers all the bases for making sure you have an up-to-the-minute record of event bookings for all your staff to see.
